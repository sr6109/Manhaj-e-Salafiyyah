Benefits of Islam

Personal Discipline: Through practices like Salah (prayer), fasting (Sawm), and charity (Zakat), Islam encourages self-discipline and self-control, aiding in personal development.

Family Stability: Islam places a strong emphasis on family values, advocating for mutual respect, care for parents, and providing a stable environment for children.

Health Benefits: Practices like fasting during Ramadan can have physical and mental health benefits when done in moderation, promoting discipline and detoxification.
